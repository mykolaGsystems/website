Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 February 2026

Who we are

Strategos Partners Limited is a software development & consultancy firm registered in England & Wales. We are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) under registration number ZC096455.

What data we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address
  • Your company name (if provided)
  • The service you are enquiring about
  • Your message or project requirements

We also use cookies to analyse site traffic. You can accept or decline cookies when you first visit our website.

Why we collect it

We collect your personal data solely to respond to your enquiry, provide you with information about our services, and to manage our business relationship with you.

Legal basis

We process your data based on your consent (when you submit the contact form) and our legitimate interest in responding to business enquiries.

How long we store it

We retain contact form submissions for up to 12 months after your last interaction with us. After this period, your data is securely deleted unless we have an ongoing business relationship.

Who we share it with

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Form submissions are processed via Formspree, which acts as a data processor on our behalf.

Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Request a portable copy of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@strategospartners.co.uk.

Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our website. These cookies are only set if you click "Accept" on our cookie banner. If you decline, no tracking cookies are placed. You can clear your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact:

Strategos Partners Limited
Email: info@strategospartners.co.uk

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office.
